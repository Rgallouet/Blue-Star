The universe

In a time where many suns have already died and birthed, Mankind still endured. grasping to the edge of its own galaxy, nothing ever stopped their expansion, until... one tiny mistake.

Hoj, Hoj, Hear the story of the lonely shuttle "Echo-42", that made his way to catalog planets and stars, proudly broadcasting to the world his research for thousands of years. His latest findings: a small blue star with a few satelites orbiting it capable of hosting a colony! 'What a beauty', should the people sing!

Alas, once the news arrived to the local Senate representative, Mr. Papouille, only fire and fury seemed to erupt from his grotesque face: "...What? A freaking blue dwarf star now!? Bloody stupid interns again playing their idle games, you can't trust them to check anything these days!!... What will it be next time, demons and dead unicorns in my garden?!".

On a turn of fate as simple as that, the peculiar finding was officially removed from the starmap, as, it's true, blue dwarf stars were always but a dream and none where ever encountered. None, until Captain Perghall, a reputable pirate and slave trader jumped on the opportunity to an uncatalog center of operations...
